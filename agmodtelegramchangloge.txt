Version 3.3.0. Changelog:

> Source code updated to v10.6.4.1.
> Self-destructing Voice Messages: Users can now send a voice message that can be listened to only once, after which it self-destructs.
> Self-destructing Video Messages: Like self-destructing voice messages, video messages disappear after the first view.
> Pause During Recording: Pause and play back a voice or a video message before it is sent.
> Updated Saved Messages.
> Limit Who You Can Receive Private Messages From.
> Message Reading Time in Private Chats.
> Colorful Calls, Thanos Snap Effect, and an Epic Update for Bots
> Bug fixes and other minor improvements.
